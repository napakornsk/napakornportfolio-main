Description

In the educational English game project inspired by subway surf and temple run, the player must non-stop running toward we can control only A and D, this game is created for the player could learn Vocaburary better and have some fun, the game's goal is that the player

  • Completes all incomplete words in the game.

  • Control only move to the left and right by pressing "A" and "D".

  • System will show an incomplete word on screen.

  • There are groups of alphabets the player must hit the right one that missing from the incomplete word.

  • When incomplete words are all completed player will win.

  • Hit the wrong alphabet the player will get knockback and decrease health by 0.3 if the player runs out of health the player will lose.

Programming

  • Player movement by scripting Rigidbody and Transform.

  • Create sound data by ScriptableObject store sounds as audio clips.

  • Play sounds when player hits specify tags using audio clip from sound data.

  • Words also control by ScriptableObject.
